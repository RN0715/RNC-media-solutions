Education Marketing

Echoes Global Education

Project Overview

RNC Media Solutions collaborated with Echoes Global Education to enhance their brand visibility and attract prospective students through comprehensive digital marketing and content creation.

Echoes Global Education is a leading education consultancy specializing in international education pathways, test preparation, and career counseling. They help students navigate global education opportunities and achieve their academic goals abroad.

Social Media Promotions

We developed targeted social media campaigns across Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn to reach students, parents, and education professionals. Our content strategy included engaging posts about study abroad destinations, scholarship opportunities, university rankings, and success stories from Echoes students.

Video Production

Our team produced high-quality promotional videos showcasing Echoes' services, including testimonials from successful students, virtual campus tours of partner universities, and informative content about the application process. These videos were optimized for social media platforms and the Echoes website.

Challenge

Echoes needed to stand out in a competitive education consultancy market and effectively communicate their expertise in international education.

Solution

We created a multi-channel approach combining authentic storytelling through video content with strategic social media targeting. Our campaigns focused on building trust, showcasing success stories, and providing valuable educational content to position Echoes as the go-to consultancy for global education aspirations.
