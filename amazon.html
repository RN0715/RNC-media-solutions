Education Marketing

Amazon College: Campus Awareness Campaign

Project Overview

RNC Media Solutions partnered with Amazon College to execute a comprehensive awareness campaign promoting their educational programs and campus offerings.

We focused on reaching prospective students through targeted social media promotions, including professional video production that showcased the college's facilities, faculty, and student life. Additionally, we organized awareness programs in schools to directly engage with potential applicants and highlight the diverse courses available at Amazon College.

Challenge

Amazon College needed to increase enrollment by effectively communicating their value proposition to a younger audience in a competitive education market.

Solution

We developed a multi-faceted campaign that combined digital marketing with grassroots outreach. Our social media strategy included engaging video content and targeted ads, while school awareness programs featured interactive sessions, presentations, and Q&A opportunities to inform students about course options and career prospects.
