Financial Services Campaign

People's Leasing & Finance PLC Promotion

Project Overview

RNC Media Solutions supported People's Leasing & Finance PLC in promoting their high-value customer engagement campaign for lease customers.

People's Leasing & Finance PLC is Sri Lanka's largest non-banking financial institution by asset base and a major subsidiary of People's Bank. For this promotion, we highlighted the company's market leadership and trust by communicating a compelling offer to prospective lease customers.

Key Offer Details

We promoted a lease campaign with a powerful reward: customers who take a lease from People's Leasing stand a chance to win free petrol for an entire year. The ad clearly stated that 25 winners would be selected every month, and each winner would receive LKR 100,000 worth of petrol per month for one year.

Challenge

The goal was to drive awareness for the lease campaign in a competitive finance market while reinforcing People's Leasing's reputation as a secure, trusted brand backed by People’s Bank.

Solution

We delivered a full activation program that combined digital promotion and field outreach. Our team developed campaign creative, produced promotional content, and distributed messaging across social media channels to reach new audiences and educate them about the lease offer and petrol prize.
